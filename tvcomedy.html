Comedy TV Shows

Binge-worthy laughs

Brooklyn Nine-Nine

Follow the hilarious misadventures of Detective Jake Peralta and his diverse, lovable colleagues as they police Brooklyn's 99th precinct. From solving crimes to navigating workplace romances, this ensemble comedy delivers endless laughs with heart.

Creators: Dan Goor, Michael Schur
Stars: Andy Samberg, Stephanie Beatriz, Terry Crews
Seasons: 8 (2013-2021)

Young Sheldon

Set in the late 1980s, this prequel to The Big Bang Theory follows 9-year-old genius Sheldon Cooper as he navigates high school in East Texas while living with his family. His extraordinary intelligence clashes hilariously with small-town life.

Creators: Chuck Lorre, Steven Molaro
Stars: Iain Armitage, Zoe Perry, Lance Barber
Seasons: 7 (2017-2024)

Modern Family

Three diverse family units navigate the trials and tribulations of modern life in this mockumentary-style sitcom. From blended families to same-sex couples with adopted children, the Pritchett clan proves that family comes in all forms.

Creators: Christopher Lloyd, Steven Levitan
Stars: Ed O'Neill, Sofía Vergara, Julie Bowen
Seasons: 11 (2009-2020)

The Good Place

Eleanor Shellstrop finds herself in the Good Place after death, but quickly realizes she doesn't belong there. With the help of her soulmate and a wise afterlife architect, she attempts to become a better person to earn her spot in paradise.

Creator: Michael Schur
Stars: Kristen Bell, William Jackson Harper, Ted Danson
Seasons: 4 (2016-2020)

Son of a Donkey

Theo comes up with chaotic schemes to save his impounded car while his dying dad will stop at nothing to find himself a new kidney. This wild comedy from the creators of Superwog blends absurd satire, family drama, and culture-clash humor in a uniquely Australian style.

Creators: Theodore Saidden, Nathan Saidden
Stars: Theodore Saidden, Nathan Saidden, Loraine Fabb
Season: 1 (6 Episodes, 2025)
