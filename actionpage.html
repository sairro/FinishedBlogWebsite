13 Hours: The Secret Soldiers of Benghazi

During the attack on the U.S. Consulate in Benghazi, Libya, a security team struggles to make sense of the chaos as they fight to save American lives. Six members of the Annex Security Team defend the diplomatic compound against overwhelming militant forces during the deadly 2012 terrorist attack. Based on true events, this intense thriller captures their heroic efforts to protect their fellow Americans during one of the longest firefights in recent military history.

Director: Michael Bay
Stars: John Krasinski, Pablo Schreiber, James Badge Dale

The Equalizer 3

Robert McCall finds himself at home in Southern Italy, but discovers his new friends are under the control of local crime bosses. When events turn deadly, McCall knows what he has to do—become his friends' protector by taking on the mafia. The retired CIA black ops operative uses his lethal skills to wage a one-man war against the criminal organization threatening the peaceful coastal town he's come to love.

Director: Antoine Fuqua
Stars: Denzel Washington, Dakota Fanning, David Denman

6 Underground

Six individuals from all around the globe, each the very best at what they do, have been chosen not only for their skill, but for a unique desire to delete their pasts to change the future. Led by a tech billionaire who faked his own death, the team becomes a vigilante squad of ghosts who operate outside the law to take down notorious criminals and terrorists that governments can't touch. Their missions are brutal, explosive, and utterly relentless.

Director: Michael Bay
Stars: Ryan Reynolds, Mélanie Laurent, Manuel Garcia-Rulfo

Extraction

Tyler Rake, a fearless black market mercenary, embarks on the deadliest extraction of his career when he's enlisted to rescue the kidnapped son of an imprisoned international crime lord. Set in the murky underworld of weapons dealers and drug traffickers in Dhaka, Bangladesh, Rake must navigate through the city's most ruthless criminals and corrupt officials. What starts as a simple extraction mission quickly spirals into a desperate fight for survival against overwhelming odds.

Director: Sam Hargrave
Stars: Chris Hemsworth, Golshifteh Farahani, Rudhraksh Jaiswal

Kill Bill: Vol. 1

The Bride wakes from a four-year coma to discover she's been betrayed by her former assassin colleagues and left for dead on her wedding day. Now she has one thing on her mind—revenge. Armed with a samurai sword and an unquenchable thirst for vengeance, she embarks on a bloody quest to hunt down the Deadly Viper Assassination Squad one by one. Stylized violence meets Tarantino's signature storytelling in this martial arts revenge epic.

Director: Quentin Tarantino
Stars: Uma Thurman, Lucy Liu, Vivica A. Fox

Kill Bill: Vol. 2

The Bride continues her quest for vengeance against her former boss and lover Bill, the reclusive bouncer Budd, and the treacherous, one-eyed Elle. Having eliminated two members of the Deadly Viper Assassination Squad, she now faces her most dangerous adversaries yet. As she closes in on Bill, long-buried secrets about their past relationship surface, revealing the true reason behind her assassination attempt.

Director: Quentin Tarantino
Stars: Uma Thurman, David Carradine, Michael Madsen
