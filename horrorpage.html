Horror

None but heart attacks

Bring Her Back

After the tragic death of her young daughter, a grief-stricken woman discovers an ancient ritual that promises to bring the dead back to life. But when she successfully resurrects her child, she realizes too late that what returned is not entirely her daughter. As dark forces begin to manifest, she must confront the horrifying truth that death should never be disturbed.

Director: Michael Chaves
Stars: Sarah Paulson, Finn Wolfhard, Vera Farmiga

The Blair Witch Project

Three film students venture into the Black Hills Forest in Maryland to document the legendary Blair Witch. Armed with cameras and camping gear, they interview locals who share chilling tales of the witch's curse. As they trek deeper into the woods, strange occurrences escalate from mysterious sounds to terrifying discoveries. Their footage, recovered a year later, chronicles a descent into madness and terror that revolutionized found footage horror.

Director: Daniel Myrick, Eduardo Sánchez
Stars: Heather Donahue, Michael C. Williams, Joshua Leonard

In the Tall Grass

Siblings Becky and Cal hear the desperate cries of a young boy calling for help from within a massive field of tall grass beside a desolate Kansas road. They venture in to rescue him, but the grass has other plans. As they become hopelessly lost and separated, they encounter others who have been trapped for much longer. Time loops, reality fractures, and a sinister force centered around an ancient rock pulls them deeper into madness. Based on the novella by Stephen King and Joe Hill.

Director: Vincenzo Natali
Stars: Patrick Wilson, Harrison Gilbertson, Laysla De Oliveira

Paranormal Activity 2

Just days after bringing their newborn son home, the Rey family experiences what they believe is a break-in. To ensure their safety, they install security cameras throughout the house. But the cameras capture something far more terrifying than burglars—a malevolent presence that has been haunting the family for generations. As supernatural activity escalates, objects move on their own, doors slam, and the baby becomes a target. This prequel reveals the dark origins of the demon that terrorized Katie in the first film.

Director: Tod Williams
Stars: Katie Featherston, Micah Sloat, Molly Ephraim
