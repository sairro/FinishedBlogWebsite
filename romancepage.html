50 First Dates

Henry Roth is a man afraid of commitment until he meets the beautiful Lucy. They hit it off and Henry thinks he's finally found the girl of his dreams until discovering she has short-term memory loss and forgets him the next day. Determined to win her over, Henry must woo her anew every single day.

Director: Peter Segal
Stars: Adam Sandler, Drew Barrymore, Rob Schneider

The Notebook

A poor yet passionate young man falls in love with a rich young woman, giving her a sense of freedom, but they are soon separated because of their social differences. Years later, their paths cross again, forcing them to confront their past and the enduring power of true love.

Director: Nick Cassavetes
Stars: Ryan Gosling, Rachel McAdams, James Garner

People We Meet on Vacation

Two best friends who couldn't be more different take a vacation together every summer. Over the years, their friendship is tested as they navigate life's ups and downs, all while harboring unspoken feelings for each other. A heartwarming tale of friendship, love, and second chances.

Director: Brett Haley
Stars: Emily Henry, Tom Blyth, Emily Bader
