Action TV Shows

Edge-of-your-seat thrills

Lupin

Inspired by the adventures of Arsène Lupin, gentleman thief Assane Diop sets out to avenge his father for an injustice inflicted by a wealthy family. Using his charisma and mastery of disguise, he pulls off a series of daring heists while staying one step ahead of the police in modern-day Paris.

Creator: George Kay
Stars: Omar Sy, Ludivine Sagnier, Soufiane Guerrab
Seasons: 3 (2021-2023)

The Night Agent

Low-level FBI agent Peter Sutherland works in the basement of the White House manning a phone that never rings—until the night it does. Thrust into a fast-paced conspiracy that leads to the Oval Office, Peter races against time to protect the President while fighting shadowy forces within the government.

Creator: Shawn Ryan
Stars: Gabriel Basso, Luciane Buchanan, Hong Chau
Seasons: 2 (2023-present)

The Recruit

Fresh CIA lawyer Owen Hendricks finds his first week on the job upended when he discovers a threatening letter from a former asset claiming to expose agency secrets. What begins as a simple legal matter spirals into a dangerous game of international intrigue, forcing the rookie to navigate deadly threats both foreign and domestic.

Creator: Alexi Hawley
Stars: Noah Centineo, Fivel Stewart, Laura Haddock
Seasons: 2 (2022-present)

The Walking Dead

Sheriff's deputy Rick Grimes awakens from a coma to discover a post-apocalyptic world dominated by flesh-eating zombies. As he searches for his family, Rick leads a group of survivors through a brutal landscape where the living can be just as dangerous as the undead. Survival demands difficult choices and moral compromises.

Creator: Frank Darabont
Stars: Andrew Lincoln, Norman Reedus, Melissa McBride
Seasons: 11 (2010-2022)
