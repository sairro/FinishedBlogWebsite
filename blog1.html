Navigate My Everyday

January 9, 2026 • 4 min read • Ailexander Kylle Cusi

Overview

As an IT student, technology is an essential part of my everyday life. I rely on different tools to help me study, travel, and stay organized. These tools make my daily tasks easier and more efficient.

Dive Deep

One IT tool I cannot live without is Google Maps. It helps me navigate unfamiliar places, find the fastest routes, and avoid traffic. As a student who often travels to school and other locations, Google Maps saves me time and reduces stress. I'm a traveler by heart I have gone to several places alone enjoying solitude, and most of the time I get lost.

I also use Google Maps to look for nearby establishments such as restaurants, stores, and study spots. Especially when I'm in an unfamiliar place to hang out, the reviews and location details help me decide where to go, even if it's a rainy day and I need a place to pass the time.

Another benefit of Google Maps is its real-time updates. It provides accurate directions and public transportation routes, making my daily commute more convenient.

Final Act

Google Maps is more than just a navigation tool for me. It helps me manage my time, move efficiently, and feel more confident when traveling to different places.
