Where Im Headed

January 9, 2026 • 3 min read • Ailexander Kylle Cusi

Introduction

Just like what travis scott say, "I can see the future" I see my future clearly taking shape, simula una hanggang ngayon kung nasaan na man ako isa lang ang ginusto ng puso ko at yun ay maging isang full stack developer within the next five years. What started as curiosity about how in the world websites and applications work has grown into a strong passion for building systems from front end to back end. ngl i enjoy the challenge of combining design, logic, and problem-solving into one complete product. Each project, lesson, and coding struggle I face now feels like a step closer to the developer I want to become. GOD knows how much i want the path im taking right now, at lahat ng hakbang ko nakasalalay at nakaalay sakanya, hindi ko alam anong gugustuhin ko if hindi itong tinatahak ko.

Discussion

Alam ko i recently talked about being a jack of all trades but if theres one thing i really wanna master i want to master both front-end and back-end development so I can build complete, functional, and user-friendly websites. i enjoy the idea of working on both the visual side of a project and the logic behind it really the thing is naeenjoy kong mahirapan and mapiga ang utak sa mga logic. dreaming to become a Full Stack Developer means I can understand the entire system, from how users interact with an website to how data is processed and stored. This role challenges me to continuously improve, and that challenge excites me. Alam ko rin na marami akong isasakripisyo para sa aking kagustuhan to reach my goal, alam kong i must constantly build and improve my technical skills. i plan to strengthen my knowledge in programming languages, web frameworks, databases, and software development principles. Aside from technical skills, I also aim to improve my problem-solving abilities which is kung saan ako pinaka mahina but step by step ill get to Where Im Headed, communication, and teamwork. I understand that being successful in IT is not just about writing code but also about collaborating with others and adapting to new technologies lalong lalo na constantly evolving ang IT field. Learning will be a continuous process for me, and I am ready to embrace it.

Takeaway

alam ko that the path toward becoming a successful full stack developer will not be easy. There will be challenges, failures, and moments of self-doubt, and even obstacles that will require a lot of sacrifice from me. However, I am committed to pushing through those moments and continuing to improve myself. Where I'm headed in the IT field is driven by passion, determination, and a clear vision of my future as well as full submission to the plan GOD has for me. With effort and perseverance, I am confident that I can turn my goals into reality.
